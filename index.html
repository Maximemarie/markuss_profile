About Me

I’m Markuss, an electronic music producer based in Montreal since 2019. Originally from France, I’ve been mixing since the age of 13, inspired by my father, who was also a DJ in the 80s.

In 2020, I began producing music with a first house project. Then I pursue in 2023 with a bigger album project named “GENOS”.
